Description: Genetiko is a digital trading card game based on the properties of genetics. The player "breeds" different cards together to create different combinations of abilities, rarities, and stats. The stats of the new card come primarily from its 2 parents by taking a selection of different genes from both parents, just like actualy genetics. However, and once again just like actual genetics, there is also the opportunity for certain genes in the card to mutate, making it unpredictably more powerful or weaker.
In addition to this aspect of the game, the player can also compete against other players in true trading card game fashion using a set of rules based around some exciting new mechanics that use a 2 line formation and a moving general that represents the player.

Role: Design Lead - I have worked on Genetiko as a leader in designing the mechanics of the game and how the game is played. As well as the layout of the game and the design of the program itself.

Language(s) Used: Java, C#

Tool(s) Used: IntelliJ, Unity, Visual Studio

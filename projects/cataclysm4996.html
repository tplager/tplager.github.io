Cataclysm 4996

Description: Cataclysm 4996 is a casual puzzle game in the same vein as games such as Unblock me or Rush Hour. The game is based in the world of Misenchanted, and, as such, the player plays as a demonologist attempting to summon hordes of demons to the world to end the Great War for the Northern Army.
The game was created during a 5 week sprint as a class project, with the help of a team of 5, including myself.
While many of its mechanics are simply borrowed from the games it is based on, it does introduce them in a novel way because it is a circle instead of the normal rectangle, which only allows the player to move horizontally and vertically.

Role: Project Lead - I worked on Cataclysm 4996 as the Project Lead, setting up meetings, organizing the structure of the project, and keeping other members of the team on task. However, I also wrote a significant part of the code for the gameplay mechanics.

Language(s) Used: C#

Tool(s) Used: Unity, Visual Studio

VS Snowman

Description: VS Snowman is a First-Person Shooter in which the player plays as a snowman competing against another AI snowman in a snowball fight. Armed with their snowball cannon and the ability to build defensive snow walls, the player must attempt to defeat their opponent before they lose all of their own health, gathering new snow to replenish their ammunition at the same time.
This game was developed for a Game Prototyping course. It was developed in 1 week as preparation for a game jam that we later participated in. To further simulate game jam conditions, development was prompted by a theme. The given theme that prompted this game was "Snow."
The most interesting aspect of this project, though, is the team composition. VS Snowman was created by a team of 6, including myself, but 3 of those team members were based in Japan and were limited in their skill with the English language. As a result, successful development of the game hinged upon all parties putting effort into communicating effectively.

Role: Developer - I worked on VS Snowman as one of 3 developers. My taks were mostly limited to implementing the defensive snow wall according to the specifications we were given.

Language(s) Used: C#

Tool(s) Used: Unity, Visual Studio, Google Sheets
